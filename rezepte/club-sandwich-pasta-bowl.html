Club Sandwich Pasta Bowl

Description

Club Sandwich Pasta Bowl: The Ultimate Twist on a Classic Favorite

Ingredients

  • 12 oz tri-color pasta
  • 8 oz block of cheddar cheese, cubed
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 cup bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1 cup lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup salami, diced (or diced ham)
  • 1 cup cooked turkey or cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning mix

How to concoct pasta bowl

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the tri-color pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking and cool the pasta.
  2. While the pasta cooks, get your ingredients prepped: cube the cheddar cheese, dice the tomato, chop the bacon, lettuce, and salami (or ham), and cut the turkey or chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a big mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise and ranch seasoning mix. Stir until smooth and well blended.
  4. Add the cooled pasta, cheddar cubes, diced tomato, bacon, lettuce, salami, and turkey or chicken to the bowl with the dressing.
  5. Gently toss everything together until all the pasta and toppings are evenly coated with the creamy dressing.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place it in the refrigerator. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to let all the flavors mingle and develop.
